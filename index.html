POC KET FUL OF GEMS

In this game, you are a successful dealer in bulk gemstones from Chicago's Jeweler's Row. You fill orders of random assortments of gems based on the total value of the order. Each type of gem has its own value. Click on the gems to build the current value to match the target value. If the value of gems exceed the target value, the order fails. When you fill or complete an order, a new order will be loaded. Beware when you get a new order, the values of the gem types will be different. To move to the next order without completing the current order, simply hit the next order button. Good luck!
